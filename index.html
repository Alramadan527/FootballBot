History

The history of soccer can be traced back centuries. Many ancient cultures had games that involved kicking a ball, but the earliest form of modern soccer originated in England in the 19th century. In 1863, the English Football Association (The Football Association) was founded as the governing body of modern soccer and began to set official rules.
